Dataset downloaded from RIGIS: http://www.rigis.org/datasets/voting-precincts

Polygons representing the voting precincts determined by Rhode Island municipalities for the 2016 election cycle.  Voting precincts are drawn at the local level by the respective City/Town. They follow the guidelines set forth in RIGL 17-11-1.
